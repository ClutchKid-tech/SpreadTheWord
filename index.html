Why We Exist

Most app agencies take 12–16 weeks to ship an MVP. They pad timelines, waterfall their way through discovery, and charge you for meetings about meetings.

We think that's broken.

We stripped out everything that slows agencies down. No 3-week discovery phases. No design-by-committee. No handoffs between six different people. One founder. One process. One timeline.

The result: what most agencies quote 12–16 weeks for, we commit to delivering in 14 days. And we put skin in the game — a portion of our fee is tied to hitting that deadline.

How a 2-week
build works

Every project follows this sequence.
No exceptions. No scope creep.
01
Strategy Sprint
Day 1–2
We define your core user, map the 3–5 screens that matter, write the technical spec, and set up your codebase. You approve the wireframes before we write a single line of code.
02
Design & Build
Day 3–10
We design the UI, build the business logic, integrate your APIs, and wire up payments, auth, and data. Daily Loom updates so you see progress in real time — not a black box for 8 weeks.
03
Ship & Measure
Day 11–14
QA, performance testing, app store submission. We set up analytics from day one so you have real user data within 48 hours of launch. Then we iterate based on what the numbers say.
